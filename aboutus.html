About Smile_Dude

Smile_Dude is a one stop destination for getting the best of everything you need in a hotel, restaurant or any food-corner. It's a place where you can book a dining for you, have a quick look of the services of any hotel, restaurant or just simply order some delicious food for yourself so that you can enjoy it at the comfort of your home.

We have tried to bring all the parts of “Hospitality Industry” on the same platform so that they can coordinate with each other perfectly and serves you better. A major thrust has been given to support and upliftment of smaller restaurant/food-corner so that they can grow better under the guidance and experience of established stakeholders.

Customers are HAPPY, service providers are HAPPY and we are blessed to make them HAPPY.
