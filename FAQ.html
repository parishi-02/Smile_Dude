FAQ
Q.1 How a person can check the services of a restaurant and make a booking for the dining?
Go to the search bar on home page and search for the restaurant using it’s name and you will get details about it’s services. To book, simply create your account on Restaurant_Dude if you haven’t and then follow the steps after clicking on “book a dining” to enter your details for booking.
Q.2 How can one contact for the customer services?
Go to ‘support’ on the top right and then to ‘contact us’ where you can either contact us directly through mobile numbers provided or can send your query to us for which you will get a response from our team within 48-60 working hours.
Q.3 What are the charges to make a booking for dining or any other purpose?
It solely depends upon the hotel or restaurant for which you are making a booking and hence charges are not fixed.
